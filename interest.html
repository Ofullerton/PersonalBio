Lacrosse

Like I mentioned on the home page my passion is playing lacrosse. My freshman year at Chico I was not able to play but fortunately I was given the opportunity to play my sophomore year. I began playing when I was in 5th grade and never looked back at any other sports. Coincidentally someone who is on my team was on my 5th grade team which shows how tight knit the community is. I think I truly grew the passion for the game in 8th grade because that is when I became really good at it. Similarly in high school I didn't play my freshman year but finally came back around to it in sophomore year. At some point in my highschool career I had decided I wanted to either play against or for the University of Oregon Ducks in Oregon. It wasn't until last year I found myself on an 8 hour bus ride to do just that. I am truly blessed to be able to play every day. These pictures included are simply me doing what I do.
